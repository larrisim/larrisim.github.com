About

Larry Chang is a versatile software developer and technical audio artist with a deep passion for creating immersive experiences through the intersection of technology, audio, and interactive media. He holds a Master of Entertainment Technology from Carnegie Mellon University and a Bachelor of Business Administration from National Taiwan University. With extensive experience in both software development and audio programming, Larry has worked on a wide range of innovative projects, from web development to virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) experiences.

Larry is proficient in a range of programming languages, including Python, C#, C, and JavaScript, and has expertise in web development, game engines, and audio tools. His technical skill set, combined with his creative approach to sound design, has enabled him to consistently push the boundaries of interactive and immersive media.

Dragonspire
(Audio Programming and Implementation)

I implemented audio content into UE5 by utilizing Unreal blueprints and custom scripting to ensure seamless integration and functionality. Leveraging Unreal's advanced MetaSounds system, along with in-house audio tools, I optimized audio performance across multiple projects, achieving a high level of precision and efficiency. This process involved fine-tuning audio assets to enhance the overall gaming experience, ensuring that sound design was immersive, responsive, and aligned with the technical demands of each project.

Beyond Van Gogh: A Life in Letters VR Experience

        

I developed and implemented an immersive audio system for Beyond Van Gogh: A Life in Letters, enhancing the exhibition's emotional and sensory impact. By carefully designing and integrating spatial audio elements, I ensured that the soundscapes complemented the visual narrative, creating a fully immersive experience for visitors. The audio system was crafted to respond dynamically to the exhibition’s flow, enveloping the audience in a multi-layered auditory journey that deepened their connection to Van Gogh’s life and art.
